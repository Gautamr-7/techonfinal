About Techon Solar

Top Solar Energy Solution Provider In Kerala

Welcome to Powergene, Kerala's top solar power solutions provider, with over 22+ years of industry experience since our inception in 2002. Our journey began with a mission to address Kerala's growing demand for consistent power supply, offering backup solutions for homes and offices. Embracing the transition to solar energy, we've partnered with industry leaders to provide perfect solar power integration services. As primary distributors for leading brands in the southern region, we ensure access to impeccable products and government subsidies, including MNRE subsidies of up to 40%. Committed to delivering excellence in both products and service, we are dedicated to shaping a brighter, sustainable future.

Solar energy reduces your electricity bills, provides clean renewable energy, increases property value, and helps reduce your carbon footprint. With government subsidies available in Kerala, solar installation becomes even more cost-effective.
Savings depend on your energy consumption and system size. Most homeowners save 50-90% on their electricity bills. With MNRE subsidies of up to 40%, your payback period can be as short as 3-5 years.
The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) provides subsidies up to 40% for residential solar installations. Additional state-level incentives may also be available in Kerala.
Typically, residential solar installation takes 1-3 days depending on system size and complexity. We handle all permits and paperwork to ensure a smooth process.
Solar panels require minimal maintenance. Regular cleaning and annual inspections are recommended. We provide comprehensive maintenance services to ensure optimal performance.
